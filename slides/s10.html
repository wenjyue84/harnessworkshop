The Prisma Engineering Philosophy

"In the AI era, the programmer's job shifts from writing the core feature to writing the definitive test suite that proves the AI's feature works."

— Prisma Engineering Masterclass
💡 Feynman Explanation

Imagine a chef who doesn't taste their own food — but they have a team of food critics with a 200-point checklist. If every critic passes it, the dish is right. The critics are your tests. The chef is your AI.

The Self-Improvement Loop

✍
You write the test suite
Define what "correct" means. Every edge case.
↓
⚡
AI writes the feature
Generates code to satisfy the tests.
↓
💥
Tests run automatically
CI/CD or hooks trigger the test suite.
↓
🔎
AI reads its own errors
Test output becomes the AI's feedback.
↓
🔄
AI iterates until all pass
Loop repeats. No human intervention.

Why This Is The Future of Programming

🎯
Tests = Specification
A passing test suite proves the feature works. No ambiguity. No "it works on my machine."
∞
Infinite Iterations, Zero Fatigue
The AI can run this loop 1,000 times overnight. A human would burn out after 10.
📋
Errors Become Training Signal
The AI reads its own test failures like a compiler — structured feedback it can directly act on.
💪
Your Leverage Multiplies
1 engineer writing a great test suite = an entire team's output. Your tests become the AI's manager.

💡 The highest-leverage skill in the AI era is not writing code — it's writing tests that force the AI to write correct code.
